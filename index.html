Biography

Don Norman is a pioneer in the field of Human–Computer Interaction. He is widely known for introducing the concept of user-centered design and usability in everyday products.

Why Human–Computer Interaction Matters

Human–Computer Interaction focuses on making systems easy to use, efficient, and accessible. Good design reduces user errors and improves overall experience.

Don Norman’s work helps designers understand how people think and interact with technology in real-world situations.

Norman’s Principles in Our Website

Don Norman is a famous HCI expert who taught us that good design should be easy to use and understand. We tried to follow some of his ideas while making this website.

1. Visibility

We made all buttons and links clear so users know what to click and what will happen next.

2. Feedback

Every time a user clicks a button or fills a form, the website gives a response so the user knows their action worked.

3. Simple Structure

Menus and pages are organized so users can find what they need easily without getting confused.

4. Error Prevention

We tried to avoid mistakes by showing clear instructions and disabling impossible options.

These are just some ways we followed Norman’s ideas to make this website user-friendly.
