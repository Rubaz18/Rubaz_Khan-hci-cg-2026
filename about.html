Who is Don Norman?

Don Norman is a well-known researcher and author in the field of Human–Computer Interaction. He focuses on how people use everyday objects and digital systems.

Contribution to HCI

His work emphasizes user-centered design, usability, and cognitive psychology. These ideas help designers create systems that are easy to understand and use.
